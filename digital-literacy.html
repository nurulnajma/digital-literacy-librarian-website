Digital Literacy Department

Department Objectives

The main objective of the Digital Literacy Department is to empower students and the academic community with essential digital skills for learning and research, while shaping responsible, media-literate, and ethical digital citizens.

Roles and Functions

This department provides training and support on digital information skills, develops digital learning materials, designs literacy modules for students, and collaborates with faculty to integrate digital literacy into the curriculum.

Digital Services Offered

  • Self-learning platforms such as interactive tutorials
  • Online consultations and digital appointment sessions
  • Chatbot and live support for quick reference
  • Guides and training for digital tools and software

Emerging Technologies in Learning

  • Artificial Intelligence (AI) for personalized search and smart content
  • Virtual Reality (VR) for immersive learning experiences
  • Augmented Reality (AR) to enrich learning materials
  • Digital Labs and virtual experimental spaces for learning
