Frequently Asked Questions

1. Who can join the digital literacy programmes?

All students, lecturers, and university staff are welcome to participate.

2. Are the programmes free?

Yes, all workshops and literacy activities are provided for free.

3. Do I need to register?

Yes. Registration can be done online via the provided form or link.

4. Where are the programmes held?

Each programme’s location will be informed through official announcements or posters.

5. Who can I contact for further questions?

You may contact Najma via the email or phone number listed on the Contact page.
